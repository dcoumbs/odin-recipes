Rosemary and Lemon Oven Roasted Chicken

Description

This weekend meal packs a delicious punch! Deep roasted chicken with herby flavors and
lemon highlights will steal the show at your dinner party!

Ingredients

  • Small roasted chicken
  • 2 lemons
  • 3 small onions
  • bundle of rosemary
  • 1 lb butter
  • kosher salt and black pepper
  • 4 garlic cloves

Directions

  1. Wipe down the chicken with a paper towel to remove excess moisture.
  2. Cut the lemons into halves and place inside the cavity of the chicken.
  3. Crush garlic cloves and make a paste with the butter. Add chopped rosemary.
  4. Rub butter mixture inside and outside the chicken. Make sure to rub underneath the skin!
  5. Place onions inside the dutch oven along with the chicken.
  6. Season everything with salt and pepper.
  7. Place into 400 degree F. oven for 1.5 hours or until the chicken is done.
  8. Rest the chicken until ready to carve. Serve with crunchy bread and steamed vegetables if desired.
