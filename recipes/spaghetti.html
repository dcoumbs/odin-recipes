Spaghetti with Roasted Broccoli

Description

Enjoy this easy weeknight meal that improves with the leftovers!
Roasted Broccoli provide a satisfying vegetable to round out this hearty dinner the whole family will love.

Ingredients

Roasted Broccoli

  • 2 heads fresh broccoli
  • olive oil
  • garlic powder
  • salt/pepper

Spaghetti

  • Angel Hair Pasta
  • 2 lbs. ground beef 85/15
  • 1 can tomato paste
  • 1 can crushed tomatoes
  • worcestershire sauce
  • 1 medium onion
  • parmesan cheese

Directions

  1. Cut the broccoli heads into small pieces.
  2. Drizzle olive oil to coat broccoli.
  3. Shake on seasonings and place onto sheet pan.
  4. Bake for 20 minutes at 400 degrees F.
  5. Fry off beef and then onions until softened.
  6. Add tomato paste and brown to develop flavor.
  7. Add crushed tomatoes to deglaze pan.
  8. Add worcestershire sauce and parmesan cheese to taste.
  9. Enjoy!
