New York Cheesecake

Description

This delicious cheesecake only requires a few ingredients and produces results any family would love.
A water bath keeps the texture moist while the graham cracker crust provides a satisfying crunch.

Ingredients

Graham Cracker Crust

  • 1.5 cups crushed graham crackers
  • 4 tablespoons melted unsalted butter
  • 4 tablespoons light brown sugar

Cheesecake Batter

  • 32 ounces full fat cream cheese
  • 2 cups white sugar
  • 0.5 cups sour cream
  • 3 large egges
  • 1 vanilla bean pod

Directions

  1. Mix together the ingredients for the graham cracker crust
  2. Pack crust into pan and bake at 450 degrees F. for 15 minutes
  3. Allow crust to cool and then combine cheesecake batter ingredients
  4. Pour batter into cool pan and place into a water bath
  5. Bake at 350 degrees F. until the center of the cheesecake wobbles slightly.
  6. Allow the cake the cool completely in the fridge for 24-48 hours.
  7. Enjoy with a strawberry sauce or fresh fruit!
